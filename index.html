About Me

My name is Gayla Cloud. I absolutely love swimming. I may not be a Michael Phelps, but I am very inspired by him and his gift. I watched every Olympic game that we raced in. It was an exciting experience, even though I was watching from the tv. I was an athlete in high school and college at SMU on a full ride scholarship. I ran track & field. I ran the 100m hurdles & 400m hurdles. For a short girl, I did alright. I currently stay active occasionally but not as regular as I use to. Eating healthy is my main challenge but I try. That is a major key to being fit.
